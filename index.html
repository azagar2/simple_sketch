To Select: Click select button and then click desired shape on the canvas. The shape's outline will become bolded. To move the selected shape, click a different point on the canvas. To deselect, click the select button again.

Polygons: To draw a polygon, click a polygon button. Clicking once on the canvas will begin a new line. Click again to draw the line and start a new one. Double click to stop drawing (closed polygon will close shape's path).
